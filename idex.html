The Volkswagen Golf is a compact hatchback that is available in the international markets in a two-door or four-door body style. Regardless of its body style, the base Golf comes powered by a 2.5-litre, five-cylinder engine that is rated at 170PS and is coupled to a six-speed automatic gearbox.
